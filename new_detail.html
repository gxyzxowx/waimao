Property: Blue Valley town
House type: three rooms, two halls and two bathrooms
Area: 102m2
Style: new Chinese style
Total cost: 320000
Designer: Fang Guanqiao
Main materials: Marble brick, wooden line, hard wrapped Jazz white marble Design Description: the style of this case is oriented to the new Chinese style. The ground is paved with gray marble brick, a large number of wooden lines are used, the sofa background wall, and the TV wall are hard wrapped. The whole space rhythm is light and bright. The entrance porch is paved with jazz white marble. This case gives up the overall framework of traditional Chinese style and adds some modern elements to make the whole space have the character of the master.
